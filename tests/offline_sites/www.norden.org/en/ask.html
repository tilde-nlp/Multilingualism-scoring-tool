Fill in our contact form below to submit your questions to The Nordic Council of Ministersâ€™ information service, Info Norden. We aim to answer your questions as quickly as possible.

Info Norden tries to answer all inquiries within five working days. The answer will be general and will usually refer to an authority in the Nordic countries who can help you. If you have questions regarding the processing of a specific case or application, or other personal matters, please contact the relevant authority directly.

Your personal information will be viewed and used only by Info Norden. We do not pass on any information to case officers or authorities.

What we canâ€™t help with

Info Norden does not provide legal advice and cannot take up a case on your behalf towards an authority.
