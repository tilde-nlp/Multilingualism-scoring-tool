Agenda
More information

26.10.20

09:00 - 17:00

1.
No plenary sessions

Since the traditional Session has been cancelled this year, there will be no plenary debates either. Nor is there a traditional agenda. Instead, several online meetings will be held during Session week. See the programme tab on the right for details. The debate on COVID-19 and the impact of the pandemic on the Nordic Region and the award ceremony for the Nordic Council prizes will both be broadcast live on 27 October.
