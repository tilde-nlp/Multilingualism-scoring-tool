Annual Session of the Nordic Council 2019

From 29 to 31 October, politicians from across the Nordic Region will gather for the 71st Session of the Nordic Council in the Swedish parliament in Stockholm. The climate, sustainability, and the involvement of young people all feature on the agenda. The Session is a Nordic summit and the largest Nordic political event of the year, where the 87 members of the Nordic Council come together with several ministers, including prime ministers.
