Nordic Food Policy Lab

The overall aim of Nordic Food Policy Lab project is to encourage the use of Nordic policy solutions to help address the food issues identified as challenges in the UN Agenda 2030 Sustainable Development Goals. Nordic Food Policy Lab will establish opportunities to advocate for innovative policies encouraging consumers to choose more sustainable food.
