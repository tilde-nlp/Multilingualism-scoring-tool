The Arctic

Since much of its territory, both on land and at sea, falls within the Arctic Circle, Â the Nordic Region is therefore strongly involved in issues that concern this unique and harsh, but also vulnerable area. The Nordic countries co-operate to improve the quality of life for the indigenous people in the northern areas and to support social and cultural development for the Arctic people.
