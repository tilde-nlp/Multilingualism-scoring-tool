Members of the Nordic Council

The Nordic Council has 87 elected members. The ongoing political work is done by committees and party groups. The Council has a Secretariat in Copenhagen and national secretariats  in the Nordic parliaments. You will find the members of the Nordic Council on this page. They can be sorted by national delegation, political group and committee.
