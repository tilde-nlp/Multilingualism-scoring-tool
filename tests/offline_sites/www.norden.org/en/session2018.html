Nordic Council's session 2018

70th Session 2018 Democracy under pressure, freedom of movement in the Nordic Region and co-operation on legislation are just some of the topics high on the agenda for the 70th Session of the Nordic Council in the Norwegian Parliament in Oslo, 30.10–1.11 2018. The Session is the Nordic Summit, the biggest political event in the Region, bringing together politicians from all of the countries, the 87 members of the Nordic Council, and a range of ministers, including the prime ministers.
